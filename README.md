# Food-Delivery-SQL-Analytics
This project analyzes a food delivery business dataset using SQL to uncover insights related to customer behavior, restaurant performance, revenue trends, delivery efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
Dataset Information
The project consists of multiple related datasets representing a food delivery platform:
-Customers
-Orders
-Restaurants
-Deliveries
-Feedback
## Business Questions Solved
1.How does monthly revenue trend over time?
2Which customers generate the highest revenue?
3.What percentage of revenue comes from top customers (Pareto Analysis)?
4.Which customers have the highest Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)?
5.Which restaurants perform best based on revenue, orders, ratings, and delivery metrics?
6.What are the most preferred payment methods?
7.How do delivery times impact customer satisfaction?
8.Which customers place repeat orders?
9.What factors contribute to restaurant success?
